## Vision

A world where landowners see rainwater as a resource to be gathered, cared for, and valued rather than a nuisance to be removed.

## Mission

Pluvii helps landowners understand how water moves across their land and turn that understanding into practical water-retention measures that reduce flood and drought risk, improve resilience, and create long-term ecological and economic value.

## Problem Statement

Many landowners and land stewards already experience flooding, drought stress, erosion, and unstable land performance, but they lack clear, site-specific guidance on what is happening on their land and what they can do about it without relying on expensive, generic, or poorly grounded advice.

## Who We Serve

Pluvii serves land stewards who are responsible for the performance and long-term health of land and who need practical ways to retain more rainwater on site.

## Ideal Customer Profiles

### Broad Opportunity

Anyone who owns land, carries out economic activity on it, and has water-related problems.

### Phase 1 Primary ICP

Private landowners with at least 1 hectare of land, economic activity tied to the land, and a clear water-related problem to solve.

### Future ICP Expansion

Potential future customer groups include:

- Farmers
- Foresters
- Watershed managers
- Municipalities

### Triggers To Buy

Customers are more likely to act when they are dealing with one or more of the following:

- Recent flooding
- Recurring drought
- Erosion issues
- Unstable yields or land productivity
- A recent land purchase
- A planned restoration or regeneration project

### Desired Outcomes

Customers want practical, measurable improvements such as:

- Less flooding
- More water availability through dry periods
- Healthier trees, soils, or crops
- Better land productivity
- Lower risk
- Higher resilience
- More biodiversity
- A more beautiful and functional landscape

## Positioning

Pluvii is a pragmatic water-retention planning and advisory partner for landowners who want to understand how water behaves on their land and know what to build to retain more of it effectively.

Its positioning should be able to speak to ecological, technical, and economic value depending on the customer’s motivation, while keeping the underlying offer consistent.

### Alternatives

Without Pluvii, customers may rely on:

- Generic consultants
- Civil engineers
- Permaculture designers
- GIS freelancers
- Trial and error
- Doing nothing

### Differentiation

Pluvii uses public data and peer-reviewed methodology to produce recommendations that are both affordable and grounded, making water-retention planning more accessible without losing rigor.

### Tone And Brand Character

Pluvii should sound:

- Pragmatic
- Grounded
- Clear
- Results-oriented

Pluvii should not sound:

- Mystical
- Luxurious
- Heavy with consultancy jargon

## Services

Pluvii provides a planning and advisory package for landowners dealing with water-related challenges on their land.

Customers receive:

- Site maps derived from available data
- Analysis of how water moves, pools, and can be retained on the property
- A written report with calculations and recommendations
- Guidance on suitable water-retention measures, including where they should go, how large they should be, and why
- A 1-on-1 session to explain the findings and support decision-making

### What We Do Not Do

Pluvii does not build, install, or manage construction. It does not replace engineering sign-off, permitting, or contractor execution. Its role is planning and guidance, closer to an architect for rainwater retention on land.
